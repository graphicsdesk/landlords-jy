503 W. 122nd St. has a total of 624 Housing Preservation and Development violations and complaints, according to city data collected from 2001 to 2025. Only 192 of the violations remain open by the city today. The rest were closed, despite residents saying the issues persist.

These are some of their complaints.

Weve had black mold citations from the city for over three years now. The city's never done anything to follow through.

Under my 10 year old's bed, one month she had a hole to the dirt, and when the guy came to fix it, they didn't put cement down. They put wood on top of dirt.

I have doctors letters from me getting bit by spiders

She had rats come to her kitchen, where they literally ate through cans of Spaghettios.

We have a step thats getting ready to collapse through and I showed the guy from the city and he wrote it off and closed the case.

We did not have hot water in the building for the whole weekend.
